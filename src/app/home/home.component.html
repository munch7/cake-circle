Our story

Cake 360 started in 2018 as a hobby and legally incorporated in 2019

What started as a banana bread and cupcakes bakery only is now a fullfledged bakery offering a wide variety of pastries for all occassions

We pride ourselves in our extremely fresh pastries with just the right amount of sugar. Our carefully and keenly crafted signature recipes give us an edge in the market allowing us to create lasting impressions for our customers

Success is a journey, not a destination. Cake 360 continues to constantly improve our produces and bring you nothing short of the best
